Walla Walla University

While an English professor at Walla Walla University from 2013 to 2022, I taught these courses for the English Department and the Honors Program:

ENGL 121 College Writing I

taught 1-2 times per year from 2013-2022

Introductory freshman composition

ENGL 122 College Writing II

taught 1-2 times per year from 2013-2022

Intermediate freshman composition

ENGL 210 Survey of English and American Literature

taught Fall 2015, Fall 2016, Fall 2018, Fall 2019, Fall 2020, Fall 2021

Survey of medieval and Renaissance English literature and the early literature of the Americas for lower division English majors and minors

ENGL 211 Survey of English and American Literature

taught Winter 2017

Survey of eighteenth-century and Romantic English literature and American literature from the Revolutionary War to the Civil War for lower division English majors and minors

ENGL 214 Themes in Literature: The Bible in English Literature

taught Winter 2014, Spring 2015, Spring 2016

Introduction to literature for general studies students themed on literary responses to the Bible

ENGL 214 Themes in Literature: Plagues, Illness, and Medicine

taught Winter 2022

Introduction to literature for general studies themed on literature about plagues and illness

ENGL 223 Research Writing

taught Fall 2013, Spring 2014

Introduction to research writing and methods for sophomore general studies students

ENGL 274 British Literature in Context: Medieval and Renaissance Literature

taught Summer 2022

Summer tour course in the United Kingdom about medieval and Renaissance literature for lower division English majors, honors students, and general studies students

ENGL 327 Research and Writing in Religion

taught Winter 2016, Winter 2017, Winter 2018, Winter 2019

Intermediate research writing and methods for upper division religion and theology majors; co-taught with a colleague in theology

ENGL 344 Medieval Literature

taught Spring 2014, Spring 2016, Winter 2020

Anglo-Saxon and Middle English literature for upper divison English majors and minors

ENGL 345 Renaissance Literature

taught Winter 2015, Fall 2018

Sixteenth- and seventeenth-century English literature for upper division English majors and minors

ENGL 360 Shakespeare at Ashland

taught Summer 2015

Summer tour course at the Oregon Shakespeare Festival in Ashland, Oregon, for upper division English majors and general studies students; co-taught with a colleague in English

ENGL 454/RELB 354 Literature of the Bible

taught Spring 2017, Spring 2019, Spring 2021

Cross-listed English and religion course on the Bible as literature for upper division English majors and general studies students

ENGL 470 Literary and Critical Theory

taught Winter 2015, Winter 2016, Winter 2017, Spring 2017, Spring 2018, Spring 2019, Spring 2020, Spring 2021, Spring 2022

Literary theory for upper division English majors

ENGL 474 Topics in British Literature: Tales of Pilgrimage and Wandering

taught Summer 2022

Summer tour course in the United Kingdom themed on travel literature for upper division English majors, honors students, and general studies students

HONR 131 Western Thought

taught Fall 2013, Fall 2014, Fall 2015, Fall 2016

Survey of classical Greek and Roman literature, philosophy, and history for lower division honors students; co-taught with a collegue in philosophy or history

HONR 132 Western Thought

taught Winter 2018, Winter 2019, Winter 2020, Winter 2021, Winter 2022

Survey of medieval and early modern literature and history for lower division honors students; co-taught with a colleague in history

HONR 243 Honors Research Writing

HONR 497 Honors Seminar
